About Us

Lifestyle brand where luxury and functionality come together in a watch. 'Aurum' means 'gold' in Latin, 'time' refers to the meaning of our creations of watches that measure the life of the moments.

Core Values

Our core values guide our brand's operations, focusing on quality craftsmanship, sustainability, and timeless design. We meticulously handcraft every watch using the finest materials, ensuring a sustainable and eco-friendly production process.

Vision

Our goal is to become a renowned luxury watch company, known for quality, sustainability, and timeless design, attracting a diverse generation of watch enthusiasts who appreciate horology and ethical practices.

Mission

To achieve this, we’re on a mission to build beautiful, premium watches that feel remote to the past but grounded in the present. Our dedication is to making every second count, so we strive to supply products that include the finest materials, the highest quality craftsmanship, and the utmost attention to detail. At Aurumtime, it is our goal to offer our customers timepieces that do much more than fulfill an accessory role: timepieces attuned to their look, their lifestyle, their values.
